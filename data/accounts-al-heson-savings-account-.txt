### Frequently Asked Questions

* **Am I eligible to open a Al Heson Savings Account?**
If you are an Omani national or an expatriate residing in Oman and above 18 years of age, you are eligible to apply for a BankDhofar Al Heson Savings Account.
* **Can I earn a guaranteed return on my deposit?**
Al Heson Account does not offer any interest on your deposits. However, BankDhofar offers several guaranteed return deposits, including Fixed Deposit, Recurring Deposit, Education saving plan  and Retirement saving plan. If you deposit your fund for a specific time period, you can earn a higher fixed rate of return.
* **What are my Debit and Credit Card options?**
You are automatically eligible for a BankDhofar ATM/Debit Card with your new Savings Account. Check our schedule of charges for the Debit card fees. For your credit card, you have the option to select [Mastercard](https://www.bankdhofar.com/cards/) or [Visa Credit Cards](https://www.bankdhofar.com/cards/) that meet your needs.
* **What other services can I access with my Savings Account?**
A BankDhofar Savings Account makes it very easy for you to avail [Housing Loan](https://www.bankdhofar.com/loans/housing-loan/), [Personal Loan](https://www.bankdhofar.com/loans/personal-loan/) and [Car Loan](https://www.bankdhofar.com/loans/auto-loan/) on competitive terms, as well as access to [Personal Accident Insurance](https://www.bankdhofar.com/accounts/al-heson-savings-account/#), [Motor Insurance](https://www.bankdhofar.com/insurance/motor-insurance/) and [Travel Insurance](https://www.bankdhofar.com/insurance/travel-insurance/).
* **What are the required documents for opening a saving account?**
BankDhofar requires the following documents
* Civil ID card for Omanis
* Passport copy and resident ID for Expats.
* Proof of address (for Omanis): Civil ID (if the address matches) 
* Proof of address (for Expats): Utility Bill **Or** Lease agreement **Or** Employer letter **Or** Bank Statement
* Address proof of permanent address (for Expats): Passport (if the address matches) **Or** Utility Bill **Or** Bank Statement
