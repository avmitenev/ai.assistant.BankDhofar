### Frequently Asked Questions

* **Am I eligible to open a Current Account?** 
If you are an Omani national or an expatriate aged 18 years or above, with your salary account at Bank Dhofar, you are eligible to apply for a BankDhofar Current Account.
* **What are the possible charges on a Current Account?** 
    * OMR 0.500 per month for Account Balance below minimum OMR 200
    * OMR 1.000 for six months for Dormant Accounts below minimum OMR 200
    * OMR 3.000 for closure of Current Account within one year
    * OMR 2.000 for 25-leaf chequebook / OMR 3 for 50-leaf chequebook
    * OMR 1.000 per Counter Cheque or Banker’s Cheque
    * OMR 5.000 for Stop Payment charge.
    * OMR 5.000 for Cancel stop payment charge.

For more information, please refer to [Tariffs & Charges](https://www.bankdhofar.com/media/0xzbbmd3/tariff_list_en_final_october23.pdf).
* **Can I earn a guaranteed return on my deposit?**
Bank Dhofar current account does not offer any financial returns on deposits, however, the bank offers a range of deposit accounts that guarantees returns, including [Fixed Deposit](https://www.bankdhofar.com/deposits/fixed-deposit-account/), [Recurring Deposit](https://www.bankdhofar.com/deposits/recurring-deposit-account/), [Long Term Deposit Account – Education](https://www.bankdhofar.com/deposits/education-savings-plan/) and [Long Term Deposit Account – Retirement](https://www.bankdhofar.com/deposits/retirement-savings-plan/).

Click the type of account to know more, or call us anytime 24x7 on [+968 24791111](tel:+96824791111).
* **What are my Debit and Credit Card options?**
You are automatically eligible for a BankDhofar ATM/Debit Card with your Current Account. You also have the option to select Mastercard or Visa Credit Cards that meet your needs. Terms and conditions may apply.

For a detailed list of the Campaign and Offers, please click [here](https://www.bankdhofar.com/cards/campaigns-offers/).
* **What other services can I access with my Current Account?**
A BankDhofar Current Account makes it very easy for you to avail [Housing Loan](https://www.bankdhofar.com/loans/housing-loan/), [Personal Loan](https://www.bankdhofar.com/loans/personal-loan/) and [Car Loan](https://www.bankdhofar.com/loans/auto-loan/) on competitive terms, as well as access to [Personal Accident Insurance](https://www.bankdhofar.com/accounts/current-account/), [Motor Insurance](https://www.bankdhofar.com/insurance/motor-insurance/) and [Travel Insurance](https://www.bankdhofar.com/insurance/travel-insurance/). Terms and conditions may apply.
* **Where can I apply for a BankDhofar Current Account?**
You can apply on [www.bankdhofar.com](https://www.bankdhofar.com), or drop in at any BankDhofar branch. Find your nearest location click [here](https://www.bankdhofar.com/branches/).
* **What are the required documents for opening a Current Account?**
*   Civil ID card for Omanis
*   Passport copy and resident ID for Expats.
*   Proof of address (for Omanis): Civil ID (if the address matches) 
*   Proof of address (for Expats): Utility Bill **Or** Lease agreement **Or** Employer letter **Or** Bank Statement
*   Address proof of permanent address (for Expats): Passport (if the address matches) **Or** Utility Bill **Or** Bank Statement
*   Salary Transfer Letter
