If you are an Omani national or an expatriate residing in Oman and above 18 years of age, you are eligible to apply for a BankDhofar High Yield Savings Account.

You can earn up to 4.25% on the Bank Dhofar High-Yield Account. Interest will be calculated on daily balance and credited every month. You can use this account as your operational account and perform transactions all our existing channels. Click [here](https://www.bankdhofar.com/treasury/deposit-rates/) to check the rates.

If you deposit your fund in High Yield account, you can earn high rate of return. BankDhofar offers a number of guaranteed return deposits, including [Fixed Deposit](https://www.bankdhofar.com/deposits/fixed-deposit-account/ "Fixed Deposit Account"), [Recurring Deposit](https://www.bankdhofar.com/deposits/recurring-deposit-account/ "Recurring Deposit Account"), [Long Term Deposit Account – Education](https://www.bankdhofar.com/deposits/education-savings-plan/ "Long Term Deposit Account – Education") and [Long Term Deposit Account – Retirement](https://www.bankdhofar.com/deposits/retirement-savings-plan/ "Long Term Deposit Account – Retirement").
