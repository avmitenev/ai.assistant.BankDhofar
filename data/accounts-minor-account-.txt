## Minor Account | Bank Dhofar

It’s never too early to have an Account! Open a Minor Savings Account today and enjoy your first banking experience.

*   Age: 13 to 18 years old
*   No minimum balance fees
*   Customized debit card
*   Flexible recurring deposit
*   Monthly financial assistance through social protection program
*   Open your child account via Intilaqa

With BankDhofar’s minor account, we aim to inculcate the importance of saving money and develop good financial habits amongst the 13 to 18-year-old that will benefit them throughout their lives. It will also allow them to set financial goals and work towards achieving. We are dedicated to providing a nurturing environment for your minor’s financial journey.

Start empowering our future generations today and give them the tools they need for a financially secure future.

A father or guardian can open a Minor’s Account, as long as the minor is between the age of 13 and 18 years.

No minimum balance is required

*   Passport copy or National ID and Birth certificate of the Minor.
*   Father/legal guardian’s ID if Omani
*   Copy of Wakala (incase of legal guardian)
*   Proof of address, you may use an electricity bill or water bill.
*   For non-Omani, Customer Resident ID and a copy of the passport visa page

Minors Debit card is restricted to OMR 50/- per transaction and OMR 200/- per month, Father/Guardian can deposit and withdraw at any time.
