* Gets 12 complimentary access to over 1000+ airport lounges in more than 300 cities around the world through the LoungeKey program. For details click [here](http://www.loungekey.com/visamena) Note: Any international POS transaction including face-to-face or online transaction of a minimum of USD 1 or equivalent yearly is required to be eligible for the lounge program. In case the cardholder do not meet the eligibility criteria then he/she will bear the cost of the visits until the minimum USD 1 international transaction is done. Card spend may take up to 15 days to register with LoungeKey.

* Download `Visa Dine & Travel' app and enjoy different discount over 200+ restaurants globally with the DragonPass exclusive airport dining program.

* Get personalised greeting and exceptional airport experience with YQ, a global network of specialist airport meet & assist service partners. Offer not valid in conjunction with other promotions. Cardholders can also book limo transfer, visa on arrival, baggage porters, etc.

For details click [here](https://www.visaluxuryhotels.com/). Offer is valid until 01 Jan 2024.

* Get up to 25% discount on rooms and suites when you use your card to book at Jumeirah Hotels & Resorts in the UAE, Maldives, Bali, UK, Spain, Kuwait, and Oman.

* Enjoy up to 35% discount on Standard Rates and up to 30% off on Retail Rates on Avis car rental. For details click [here](https://www.avisworld.com/visa)

* Get 10% discount on Budget car rental globally. For details click [here](https://www.budgetinternational.com/visa)
