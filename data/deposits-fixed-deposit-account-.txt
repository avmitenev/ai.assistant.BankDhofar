### Frequently Asked Questions

* **Am I eligible to open a BankDhofar Fixed Deposit Account?**
If you are an Omani national or an expatriate residing in Oman and above 18 years of age, with the ability to make a minimum deposit of OMR 1000, you are eligible to apply for a BankDhofar Fixed Deposit Account.

* **What is the interest rate and how is it calculated?**
The interest rate is based on prevailing market rates. Once the interest is offered to you, the interest rate does not change during the tenure of the deposit unless the deposit is broken. Your interest earning is calculated based on the tenure of your deposit and credited to your account at your selected frequency.

* **Can I invest in currencies other than OMR?**
Yes. In addition to Omani Rial, you have the flexibility to invest in major foreign currencies – like US Dollars, Euros, British Pounds, Swiss Francs and Australian Dollars.

* **Is there a penalty for withdrawing funds prematurely?**
Yes, a penalty of 1% will be charged if the Fixed Deposit funds are withdrawn prematurely and customer will only earn interest for the tenure for which the Fixed Deposit was actually maintained at the normal current market rate.

* **Where can I apply for a BankDhofar Fixed Deposit Account?**
You can open a Fixed Deposit online from the BankDhofar app. You can apply on www.bankdhofar.com, or drop in at any BankDhofar branch. Find your nearest location click here.

* **What are the required documents for opening a Fixed Deposit?**

If you are an existing customer,

* Civil ID card for Omanis
* Passport copy and resident ID for Expats.

If you are new to BankDhofar,

* Civil ID card for Omanis
* Passport copy and resident ID for Expats.
* Proof of address (for Omanis): Civil ID (if the address matches)
* Proof of address (for Expats): Utility Bill Or Lease agreement Or Employer letter Or Bank Statement
* Address proof of permanent address (for Expats): Passport (if the address matches) Or Utility Bill Or Bank Statement
