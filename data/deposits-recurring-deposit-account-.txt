### Frequently Asked Questions

* **Am I eligible to open a BankDhofar Recurring Deposit Account?**
The BankDhofar Recurring Deposit Account is available for all Omani nationals and expatriate residents of the Sultanate. You may be of any age.

* **What is the interest rate and how is it calculated?**
You can earn interest rate up to 5% based on your deposit tenure. For the current rate, click [here](https://www.bankdhofar.com/treasury/deposit-rates/) or please call us on [24791111](tel:24791111).

* **Am I eligible for an ATM card with the Recurring Deposit Account?**
No as BankDhofar Recurring Deposit Account does not entitle you to an ATM, debit or credit card. It is not possible to issue debit card for recurring deposit account.

* **Is there a minimum amount monthly deposit amount?**
Yes. The minimum monthly deposit for a BankDhofar Recurring Deposit Account is OMR 0.500 for daily plan, OMR  5 for weekly plan, and OMR 10 for monthly, education and retirement plan.

* **Is there a penalty for withdrawing funds prematurely?**
Yes, a penalty of 1% will be charged if the deposit funds are withdrawn before the due date, a 1% penalty is calculated in addition customer will only earn interest for the tenure for which the deposit was actually maintained at the normal market rate. To a review of the interest rate based on the actual duration please [click](https://www.bankdhofar.com/treasury/deposit-rates/) on the link.

* **Where can I apply for a BankDhofar Recurring Deposit Account?**
You can apply on www.bankdhofar.com, or drop in at any BankDhofar branch. Find your nearest location click [here](https://www.bankdhofar.com/branches/).
