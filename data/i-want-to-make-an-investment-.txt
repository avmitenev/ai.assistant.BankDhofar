## Bank Dhofar - Make an Investment

Want higher returns without the risk?

Read on.

A BankDhofar Fixed Deposit Account is one of the safest ways to invest your hard-earned money. Just choose the tenure of your deposit – and sit back.

**Here's how to apply...**

Visit your nearest BankDhofar branch (Click [here](https://www.bankdhofar.com/branches/) for locations) – or leave us your details and we’ll get back to you soon.

**Documents Required to open a Deposit**

If you are an existing customer,

*   Civil ID card for Omanis
*   Passport copy and resident ID for Expats.

If you are new to BankDhofar,

*   Civil ID card for Omanis
*   Passport copy and resident ID for Expats.
*   Proof of address (for Omanis): Civil ID (if the address matches) **Or** Utility Bill **Or** Lease agreement **Or** Employer letter **Or** Bank Statement
*   Proof of address (for Expats): Utility Bill **Or** Lease agreement **Or** Employer letter **Or** Bank Statement
*   Address proof of permanent address (for Expats): Passport (if the address matches) Or Utility Bill Or Bank Statement

**Remember this...**

*   Minimum investment amount is OMR 1000
*   Penalty of 1% may be charged on withdrawn prematurely
*   You can avail a loan against your Fixed Deposit
