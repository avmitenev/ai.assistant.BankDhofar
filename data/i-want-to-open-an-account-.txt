## BankDhofar Account Information

Do you want to Open an Account?

Begin here.

A BankDhofar Accounts opens a world of convenience and possibilities. Whether you need an account for daily transactions, cheque services, optimal returns on your funds, or access to services like borrowing, debit and credit cards, term deposits and insurance options – you’ll find it all here.

**What you need to do first...**

If you have no relationship with BankDhofar, simply download our Intilaqa app and open an account instantly from the app. Click here [https://www.bankdhofar.com/intilaqa-download/](https://www.bankdhofar.com/intilaqa-download/). If you already have an account with BankDhofar, login to the app and open another account instantly on our app. Click here [https://www.bankdhofar.com/mobile-banking-download/](https://www.bankdhofar.com/mobile-banking-download/).

**Remember to keep the following documents ready...**

**Customer Type** | **ID Proof** | **Proof of Residence address** | **Proof of Permanent Address** 
------- | -------- | -------- | --------
Omani | Civil ID | Civil ID (if it matches) | Not required
Expat | Civil ID **And** Passport | Utility Bill **Or** Lease agreement **Or** Employer letter **Or** Bank Statement | Passport (If it matches)
Minor / Child(Omani) | Civil ID **or** Passport **or** Birth Certificate | Not required | Not required
\-   Housewife | As per nationality | As per nationality | As per nationality
\-   Students | As per nationality | As per nationality | As per nationality
\-   Child /Minor (Expat) | As per nationality | As per nationality | As per nationality
\-   Unemployed | As per nationality | As per nationality | As per nationality
\-   Free lancer | As per nationality | As per nationality | As per nationality

Additionally, Salary transfer letter is required if you want to open a current account.
