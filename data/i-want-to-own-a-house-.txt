## Bank Dhofar - Own a House

Ready to own your dream home?

Start here.

Whether you want to buy land or a house, construct or renovate your home, or transfer a Home Loan from another bank, we help fulfil your needs with zero hassles, quick processing and competitive rates.

**What you need to do first...**

Visit your nearest BankDhofar branch (Click [here](https://www.bankdhofar.com/branches/) for locations) – and our Home Loan Advisor will help you with the rest.

**Remember to keep the following documents ready...**

* Most recent salary certificate
* Copy of ID card / Resident card
* Passport-size colour photo
* Copy of approved construction plan from municipality
* Valuation report from approved valuators
