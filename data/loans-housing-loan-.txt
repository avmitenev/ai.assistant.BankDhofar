If you are an Omani national over 18 years of age earning a salary of at least OMR 300 per month, you are eligible to apply for a BankDhofar Home Loan. Home loans is also available to expatriates residing in Oman.

Drop in and talk to our expert Home Loan advisors. They will help you find and apply for the most appropriate type of loan based on your need and your financial status.

BankDhofar offers a range of loans based on your needs, including:

*   Purchase of a residential land
*   Purchase of a completed or under construction, house or apartment
*   Construction of a new house
*   Extension, improvement or renovation of a house
*   Takeover (buy-out) of a housing loan from other banks.

To apply for a home loan, visit your nearest branch with the below documents. Our Home Loan Advisor will help you with the rest.

*   Filled home loan application
*   Most recent salary certificate
*   Copy of ID card / Resident card
*   Copy of approved construction plan from municipality
*   Valuation report from approved valuators
*   Six month bank statement
*   A salary transfer letter
*   Liability letter ( if any)
