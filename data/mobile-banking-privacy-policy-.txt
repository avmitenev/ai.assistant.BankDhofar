**Permission / Feature used in application**

**Usage Description**

Phone

We use this permission to identify your device information and enable the user for easier/preferred login

SMS

We use this permission  
- To autofill OTPs you receive via SMS on your device.

Storage

We use storage access  
- Downloading your statements and transaction receipts to your local device storage.  
- uploading images for your profile/ beneficiaries.

Camera/ Gallery

We use this camera / gallery access  
- To scan the QR codes for mobile payments and transactions.  
- To scan Debit & Credit card information to retrieve cards transaction details

- To scan the cards and passports for Mobile Banking on boarding process.  
- To capture the pictures or access gallery for the profile photo, beneficiary photo while using the application.

Contact

We use this contact  
- To send money to any contact from your contact book.  
- To show you the list of your contacts that you can make a recharge for.  
- To register for Mobile Payments using mobile number from contact list.

Locations

We use this location information to identify and display nearest ATM, Branch and CDM with distance

IMEI/SECURE.ANDROID_ID

We use this information to identify & tie up for the uniqueness of the device that belongs to the user. Based on this information application asks user to register or login on launch of the application.

Phone Number / Mobile Number

We use this information to identify the customer, register for the banking services usage and communicate SMS messages.
