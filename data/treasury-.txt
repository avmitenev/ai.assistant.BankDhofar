## Bank Dhofar Treasury Services

BankDhofar's treasury division provides a comprehensive suite of treasury and investment products to the retail, corporate, commercial and government institutions. They range from plain remittances to structured products. The markets range from money markets to interest rates, FX and NDF. We are Oman's leading market maker in the local currency and swaps.
