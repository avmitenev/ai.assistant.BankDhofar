## Bank Dhofar Whistleblowing Policy

BankDhofar’s ‘Whistle Blowing Policy’ is a key element to protect the integrity and reputation of Bank Dhofar. By creating an environment of trust and maximum protection, we want to encourage you to come forward and report cases of irregularities, fraud, corruption, coercion, harassment and collusion without fear of reprisal or retaliation.
